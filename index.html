General information

Kokshetau (Kazakh: Көкшетау, romanized: Kökşetau; [kɵkʂʲetɑw] lit. 'Blueish Mountain/Smoky-Blue Mountain'; Russian: Кокшета́у, IPA: [kəkʂɛtaʊ]), formerly known as Kokchetav (Russian: Кокчета́в, [kəktɕʲetav]; the official name between 1868–1993) is a city in northern Kazakhstan and the capital of Akmola Region
